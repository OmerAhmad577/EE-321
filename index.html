05 / UNDER THE SKIN

Three domains, engineered as one

SYS.01

Mechanical

The vehicle features a custom-engineered, reinforced steel tubular “exoskeleton” chassis — inspired by the Ariel Atom 4 — designed to serve as both the structural heart and the aesthetic body. This lightweight 2-seater prototype uses a rear-wheel-drive configuration to deliver a peak of 50 kW (74 hp) and 160 Nm of torque, enabling 0–100 km/h in under 4 seconds. To optimize high-speed stability, the design includes a retractable rear wing and a Drag Reduction System (DRS), alongside a differential setup tailored for spirited driving.

  • EXOSKELETON CHASSIS
  • RWD
  • RETRACTABLE WING + DRS
  • 0–100 <4.0 s
SYS.02

Electrical

The electrical architecture centers on a high-voltage powertrain built around a 50 kW peak PMSM motor and a 12 kWh battery pack optimized for high-performance discharge. For safety, the design includes an active cooling system for the onboard electronics and a multi-node emergency-stop network for instant battery isolation. A BMS manages all auxiliary units, supported by a 2 kW off-board charging solution compatible with standard infrastructure.

  • 50 kW PMSM
  • 12 kWh HV PACK
  • CUSTOM BMS
  • E-STOP NETWORK
  • 2 kW CHARGING
SYS.03

ECU

The vehicle’s brain is a custom-built Electronic Control Unit executing high-precision Field Oriented Control (FOC) for the motor, while managing auxiliary systems such as safety lighting and active aero. It runs a software suite with Eco, Normal, and Sport drive modes. For monitoring, a radio-based telemetry link streams real-time GPS, RPM, voltage, and temperature data. The system also maintains active over-current protection to safeguard the high-voltage powertrain.

  • FIELD ORIENTED CONTROL
  • ECO / NORMAL / SPORT
  • RADIO TELEMETRY
  • ACTIVE AERO CTRL
  • OVER-CURRENT PROTECTION
